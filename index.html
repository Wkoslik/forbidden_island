Overview

  • Collect the treasure cards.
  • Exchange FOUR treasure cards for an artifact.
  • Escape the island before the island sinks.

Game Play

The game is divided into two game states: The Player turn and the Island turn.

The Player always goes first followed by the Island.

Player Turn:

  • You have three actions. Those actions can be:
    • Moving
    • Exchanging treasure cards for an artifact.
    • Unflooding location tiles.
  • After your three actions, draw two cards from the treasure deck.
    • If you pull a "Waters Rise" card, the water level increases and the flood discard pile is immediately shuffled and put on top of the Flood Draw pile.
  • Collect 4 treasure cards (of the same treasure) and exchange them for the artifact. (hint: your hand limit is 5)
  • Make it to Fools Landing and fly off with a "Helicopter Lift" before the island sinks.

Island Turn

  • The Island will flood the number of tiles equal to water level.
  • If an already flooded tile is pulled, that tile sinks into the sea and is no longer usable by the player.

Ways you can lose:

  • Fools Landing sinks
  • The artifact location sinks
  • The Water Level reaches the skull and cross bones
